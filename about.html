始终保持一颗好奇心，勇敢的去尝试新鲜的事物。试一试，说不定就成了呢。
​每个优秀的人都会有一段沉默的时光。
在这段时间光里付出了很多努力，却得不到想要的结果；
我们把它叫扎根！
世人只见我绽放时的美丽，却不见还是种子时的我在土里度过的黑暗岁月。
那一段时光，是付出了很多努力，忍受孤独和寂寞，不抱怨，不诉苦，日后说起时，连自己都能被感动。
我曾经很害怕，怕什么？怕自己一无是处，怕自己没能力，最怕努力得不到收获。
无论结果怎么样，唯有持续扎根，做到问心无愧。

—— 有一段沉默的时光叫扎根

Always maintain a curiosity, bravely try new things. Give it a shot, and you might just succeed.
​Every outstanding individual has a period of silence.
During this time, a lot of effort is put in, but the desired results are not achieved.
We call this rooting!
People only see the beauty of my blooming, but they don't see the dark years I spent in the soil when I was still a seed.
During that time, I put forth a lot of effort, endured loneliness and solitude, without complaints or grievances. When I talk about it later, even I am moved by it.
I used to be very afraid, afraid of what? Afraid that I was worthless, afraid that I was incapable, most afraid of not getting results from my efforts.
Regardless of the outcome, only continuous rooting can lead to a clear conscience.

—— A Silent Period Called Rooting
